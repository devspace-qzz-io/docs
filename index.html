DevSpace Documentation

Technical documentation for the DevSpace curated developer namespace.

Welcome to the DevSpace documentation. Here you'll find everything you need to know about requesting, configuring, and managing your free developer subdomain.

What is DevSpace?

DevSpace is a manually reviewed subdomain registry providing free username.devspace.qzz.io subdomains for developers. All requests are reviewed to ensure quality and prevent abuse.

Quick Start

New to DevSpace? Follow these steps to get your subdomain up and running:

  1. Review the requirements

    Make sure your project meets our hosting requirements and content guidelines.

  2. Prepare your site

    Deploy your site to an approved hosting provider (GitHub Pages, Vercel, Netlify, etc.).

  3. Submit your request

    Fork our registry repository and submit a pull request with your subdomain configuration.

  4. Wait for review

    Our team will review your request manually. This typically takes 1-3 business days.
